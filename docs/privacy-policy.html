Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 11, 2024

Welcome to Algogo ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Progress data (completed lessons, quiz scores, flashcard reviews)
  • App preferences and settings

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Track your learning progress locally on your device
  • Improve our App and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements (for non-subscribed users)

3. Data Storage

Your learning progress and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain user accounts or store personal data on external servers. Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store: For in-app purchases and subscription management. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Advertising

The free version of our App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. You can remove ads by purchasing a subscription. We request non-personalized ads to minimize data collection, but some device information may still be collected by the ad network.

6. In-App Purchases

Our App offers subscription options to remove advertisements. All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is intended for general audiences learning computer science concepts. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security settings.

9. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your progress data by uninstalling the App
  • Reset your progress within the App settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
