Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 17, 2025

Welcome to Algogo ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers.

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Learning progress (completed learn sections, reviewed flashcards)
  • Quiz scores and high scores
  • Badges and achievements earned
  • XP (experience points) and study streaks
  • Terms acceptance status
  • Subscription status (managed by Apple)

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

2. No Tracking

Algogo does not track you. We do not:

  • Use any analytics or tracking SDKs
  • Collect device identifiers for advertising
  • Share data with third parties for tracking purposes
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Build user profiles or conduct behavioral advertising

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Display your learning progress within the App
  • Track which flashcards you've reviewed and lessons you've completed
  • Calculate and display your XP, streaks, and badges
  • Remember your subscription status for content access

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

4. Data Storage

All your learning data is stored exclusively on your device using secure local storage. We do not:

  • Maintain user accounts or databases
  • Store any data on external servers
  • Sync your data to the cloud
  • Have access to your progress or usage data

If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated with the App is:

  • Apple App Store: For processing in-app purchases and managing subscriptions. Apple handles all payment processing. We do not receive or store your payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles your data.

6. In-App Purchases

Algogo offers an optional "Algogo Pro" monthly subscription to unlock premium content. Key points:

  • All purchases are processed securely by Apple's App Store
  • We do not collect, see, or store your payment information
  • Subscription status is managed by Apple and checked locally by the App
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your App Store Account Settings

7. Children's Privacy

Algogo is an educational app suitable for general audiences learning computer science concepts. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App contains no user accounts, no data collection, and no tracking.

8. Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted:

  • Your data is as secure as your device
  • We recommend using device passcodes and keeping your device software updated
  • There is no risk of server breaches affecting your data

9. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete all data: Uninstall the App to permanently remove all stored data
  • Manage subscriptions: Go to App Store Account Settings to manage or cancel

Since we don't collect your data, there is no data to request, export, or have us delete from our servers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date." Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
